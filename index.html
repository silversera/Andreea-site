I bring my background in modern dance and choreography into my fitness classes, where rhythm, musicality and energy shape the overall experience. I have worked professionally as a dancer and choreographer with well-known Romanian artists, performing on stage and contributing to official music video productions, including collaborations with Andra, Deepcentral, and Fly Project, and has danced in internationally recognised venues such as Amnesia Ibiza

This professional experience allows me to design dynamic, rhythm-led fitness sessions that are accessible for beginners while still offering progression and challenge for more advanced clients.
